# ANCHOR: WHAT A WIN LOOKS LIKE

## PURPOSE
Define winning in judicial terms and prevent emotional burnout

## THE WIN
Winning is **correction and trajectory**, not domination.

- Status quo loses immunity
- Court oversight restored
- Ramped custody begins
- Financial leverage collapses
- Case becomes boring

## WHAT WINNING IS NOT
- Public scolding
- Moral vindication
- Instant 50/50
